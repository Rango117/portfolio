First Person Shooter

First-person shooter (FPS) is a video game genre centered around gun and other weapon-based combat in a first-person perspective.
The player experiences the action through the eyes of the protagonist. The genre shares common traits with other shooter games, which in turn makes it fall under the heading action game.
