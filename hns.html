Hack 'n' Slash

Hack and slash, or hack and slay (H&S or HnS), refers to a type of gameplay that emphasizes combat.
The gameplay is relatively simple, there is one protagonist, a bunch of close combat weapons that tend to do a lot of damage, a few ranged attacks that do little damage, and usually a colorful array of thousands and 
thousands of enemy mooks to hack and slash through until you get to the oversized bossfight.
